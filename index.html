Explore The SAMC

Sainik Awasiya Mahavidyalaya Chainpur, Chitwan (SAM Chitwan) is an English medium co-educational institution run by the Welfare Planning Directorate of Nepalese Army. SAM Chitwan is a semi-residential type of school. Whereas all the male students are residential (staying in the hostel inside the school campus), the female students are day boarders (only taking classes during school study hours). SAM Chitwan was inaugurated by General Chhatra Man Singh Gurung, the then Chief of the Army Staff, Nepal Army in a midst of an auspicious gathering of the senior Army officials, teachers, students, parents and other dignitaries on 17th July, 2012 (02nd Shrawan, 2069).
